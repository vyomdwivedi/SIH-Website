About The Project

Our team consists of 3 People and here we have proposed aur project which automates the transportation of containers which are ones placed on the truck. The bottom of the container would be fitted a RFID card which would be scanned by the RFID scanner placed at the top face of the truck where the container would be kept. According to the code of the card the truck will automatedly go to the warehouse assigned with that card's code.
